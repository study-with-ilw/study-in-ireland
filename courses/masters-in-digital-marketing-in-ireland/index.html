Masters in Digital Marketing in Ireland

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or brands via one or more forms of electronic media.Digital marketing is the strategy and processes that connect advertisers with their audiences across digital channels. An advertisement itself is a piece of creative shared via digital inventory - the space a publisher makes available for advertisements on its platform.

MSc in Digital Marketing in Ireland extends beyond internet marketing to include channels that do not require the use of the internet. Masters in Digital Marketing in Ireland includes mobile phones (both SMS and MMS), social media marketing, display advertising, search engine marketing, and any other form of digital media. In MSc in Digital Marketing in Ireland, you will learn the digital types of marketing, such as Pay per Click PPC, Adwords, social media, SEO, web advertising and also strategies to help you market effectively through the Internet.

  • Institute of Technology: 60% or 6.0 (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90.
