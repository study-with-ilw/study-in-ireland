Masters in Game Design in Ireland

So, what is game design? Game design is a subset of the field of video game development. Game design is a field with a broad focus. As such, the skills of a game designer are drawn from the fields of computer science and programming, creative writing and graphic design.

Games developers programme games and specialize in a particular area such as design, programming, animation, audio, editing or production. Game Design & Development in Ireland is an ever-evolving and multidisciplinary field which focuses on a variety of specialties including programming, game design, audio and art.
