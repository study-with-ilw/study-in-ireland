Masters in Sports Management in Ireland

Masters in Sports Management in Ireland professionals oversee the business and promotional concerns of athletic organizations or athletes. Depending on the position, job duties can include negotiating contracts and monitoring the finances of teams and athletes.The MSc in Sports Management in Ireland has an emphasis on facility management, sports information, and business features of sports within culture, intercollegiate, interscholastic, and professional sports.
