Masters in Cloud computing in Ireland

Masters in Cloud Computing in Ireland will appeal to computer science or engineering graduates as well as ICT industry practitioners, system administrators, and those with an interest in gaining practical experience in cloud computing.Masters in Cloud Computing in Ireland, one is introduced to the Cloud models of Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS) and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS).

MSc in Cloud Computing in Ireland increases the understanding of the elasticity, resiliency, on-demand and measured usage that Cloud Computing can provide to the business and gives an insight about Cloud providers and their tools and applications, for example, Amazon Web Services, Google Apps, Microsoft Azure.

Best Universities in Ireland to Study Masters in cloud computing :

  • Dublin City University
  • Technological University Dublin (Tallaght)
  • Letterkenny Institute of Technology
  • National College of Ireland
  • Dublin Business School

Typical Academic Score in Undergraduate Degree and English Language Requirement :

  • Universities: 65% or 6.5 CGPA (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 92.
  • Institute of Technology: 60% or 6.5 (on 10) and IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 79.
  • Colleges: 55% or 5.5 (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90.

Universities do not prefer more than 5 backlogs. However, if you have more backlogs, there are always options. Contact us if you want us to assess your profile - it's a free service!

Course Fee for Masters in cloud computing in Ireland :

Typical fee range:

Universities: €10,000 to €18,000.

Institutes of Technology and Colleges: €10,000 to €14,500.
