Masters in Construction Project Management in Ireland

A construction project manager is responsible for planning, coordinating, budgeting and supervising projects from the beginning to the end. In short, a construction project manager has to take care of the following: Put together the budget and negotiate cost estimates. Arrange the work timetables.

Construction project management is a complex field, requiring knowledge in many different areas like finance, mediation, law, business, and more.

Therefore, A construction manager is paid more than a civil engineer and a structural engineer. Masters in Construction Project Management in Ireland helps us cover a number of roles including; site manager, contracts manager, construction estimator, building project manager and building surveyor.MSc in Construction Project Management in Ireland will prepare you for a career in the financial, business and management sectors.

Best Universities in Ireland to Study Masters in Construction Project Management:

  • Technological University Dublin (Dublin)
  • Cork Institute of Technology
  • Waterford Institute of Technology
  • Institute of Technology, Carlow
  • Limerick Institute of Technology

Typical Academic Score in Undergraduate Degree and English Language Requirement :

  • Universities: 65% or 6.5 CGPA (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 92.
  • Institute of Technology: 60% or 6.0 (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 79.
