Masters in Architechture in Ireland

An architecture description is a formal description and representation of a system, organized in a way that supports reasoning about the structures and behaviors of the system. A system architecture can consist of system components and the sub-systems developed, that will work together to implement the overall system. Masters in Architecture in Ireland develops a highly visual, creative, technical and design-based skill set.

There is lots of room for specialization and career development after Master's in Architecture in Ireland. Architectural Conservation, Environmental Architecture, Digital Architecture, Building Automation, Environmental Planning, Housing, Industrial Design, Regional Planning, Rural Architecture, Town and Country Planning, Sustainable Architecture, Transportation Planning and Design.

  • University College Cork
  • Technological University Dublin (Dublin)
  • University of Limerick
