Masters in Computer Science in Ireland

Ireland is one of the most sought after destinations to study computer science. Ireland is the largest exporter of software, computer and IT services after the US. Most top giant tech companies have their European headquarters in Ireland. For instance, Apple, Google, HP, IBM, Twitter, Facebook, Amazon, Infosys, TCS, 100s more.

Best Universities in Ireland to Study Masters in cloud computing :

  • University College Dublin
  • Trinity College Dublin
  • Dublin City University
  • Maynooth University
  • University College Cork
  • National University of Ireland, Galway
  • Technological University Dublin

Typical Academic Score in Undergraduate Degree and English Language Requirement :

  • Universities: 65% or 6.5 CGPA (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 88.
  • Institute of Technology: 60% or 6.0 (on 10) and IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 79.
  • Colleges: 55% or 5.5 (on 10) and IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 79.

Universities do not prefer more than 5 backlogs. However, if you have more backlogs, there are always options. Contact us if you want us to assess your profile - it's a free service!

Course Fee for Masters in Computer Science in Ireland :

Tuition fees for MSc in Computer Science in Ireland starts from €10,000.

Typical fee range:

Universities: €15,000 to €24,000.

Institutes of Technology and Colleges: €10,000 to €12,500.
