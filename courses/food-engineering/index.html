Masters in Food Engineering in Ireland

Food engineering is a multidisciplinary field which combines microbiology, applied physical sciences, chemistry and engineering for food and related industries. Food engineers provide the technological knowledge transfer essential to the cost-effective production and commercialization of food products and services.The manufacture of food and drink products is Ireland's most important indigenous industry with a turnover approaching €24 billion.Food engineers in Ireland play a key role in the food industry by making groundbreaking innovation or by incremental improvements. Many of the most salient challenges facing our world are related to food.

The future food industry will need to meet increasing demands for improved health and wellness of consumers through the development of functional foods for specific consumer groups.he ME Biosystems and Food Engineering in Ireland is aimed at Engineering (BE) and Engineering Science (BSc) graduates who wish to pursue a career as a professional engineer in agricultural, food, environmental or related industries.The Master in Food Engineering in Ireland provides a comprehensive coverage of bioprocess and food manufacturing systems engineering.
