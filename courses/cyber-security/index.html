Masters in Cyber Security in Ireland

Cybersecurity is an essential need for a modern society in which information technology and services pervade every aspect of our lives. Cybersecurity has the fastest growth rate among all areas of IT, with the labour market encountering a severe workforce shortage in this field.Ireland is uniquely placed to benefit from increased global investment, it has an opportunity to position itself as a world class cyber security practices, solutions and investment hub.

#entryRequirements

Typical Academic Score in Undergraduate Degree and English Language Requirement :

  • Universities: 65% or 6.5 CGPA (on 10) and IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 88.
  • Institute of Technology: 60% or 6.0 (on 10) and IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 79.
  • Colleges: 55% or 5.5 (on 10) and IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 79.

Universities do not prefer more than 5 backlogs. However, if you have more backlogs, there are always options. Contact us if you want us to assess your profile - it's a free service!

#tutionFees

Course Fee for Masters in Cybersecurity in Ireland :

Tuition fees for MSc in Cybersecurity in Ireland starts from €10,000.

Typical fee range:

Universities: €10,000 to €18,000.

Institutes of Technology and Colleges: €10,000 to €14,500.
