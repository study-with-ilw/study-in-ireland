We are Ireland Education Specialists

Welcome to Study Ireland

We have been guiding students to study abroad since 2002.

We are one stop consulting agency for course and college selection for students considering to study in Ireland at Foundation, Undergraduate and Postgraduate level. Our core is ‘Total Solution’ for students - helping you find answers from end to end; on what to study, where to study, what's the process and much more.

With our in depth knowledge and understanding of Ireland's education system, industry and economic scenario, we offer total guidance to students to make well informed decisions across every stream - IT, Data Science, Machine Learning, AI, Management, Marketing, Science and Medicine, Pharmacy, Technology and Engineering, Mathematics, Human Resource Management, Media, Films, Language & Communication, Business and Entrepreneurship, Accountancy, Banking and Finance, Consulting, and many more.

Students benefit by our close association with the colleges and universities in Ireland by getting/enjoying easy accessibility to information about courses and colleges. Study Ireland is a free advisory consultancy to students applying to Ireland

Official Representative of Universities in Ireland

Study Ireland has direct communication with Irish universities, meaning your application will be processed quicker and more effectively than applying on your own. As representatives of the University, we receive regular updates from Universities about scholarships, changing entry requirements, etc.

Ireland is home to some of the topmost Universities such as Trinity College Dublin, University College Dublin, University College Cork, University of Limerick, Maynooth University, NUI Galway, and more. All Universities visit us multiple times in a year where students get the opportunity to meet them personally.

How do we help you to study in Ireland

Study Ireland provides free counseling and application support to Indian students applying to study in Ireland.

Our Irish education experts are fully experienced and trained by trusted university partners and are here to help guide you through each step of the application process to any Irish university.

Our counseling services will include everything:

  • University shortlisting
  • Editing SOP, essays, resume, scholarship essays, etc.
  • Applications
  • Admission
  • Visa guidance
  • Scholarship applications
  • Connecting with other students
  • Value ads: Education loans, planning finances, accommodation selection, etc

Track Record

  • Several of our students have received 70% to 100% scholarship
  • Our admission and visa success rate is near 100%
