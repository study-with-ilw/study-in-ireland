Tuition Fees

Master’s degree course fee in Dublin City University is likely to differ drastically across courses and also due to the fee-paying status of the student. Domestic students and students from the European Union are highly likely to pay lower fees when it comes to the postgraduate tuition fees at Dublin City University.

A rough estimate can be drawn when it comes to graduate programs tuition fee at Dublin City University. For most programs, the postgraduate tuition fee in Dublin City University is likely to be somewhere from €13,000 to €20,000.

Bachelor’s degree course fee in Dublin City University is subject a lot of the same provisions and features as was talked about postgraduate studies above. Not only do non-EU students have to pay exorbitantly more than most domestic students in the country but bachelors programs tuition fee in Dublin City University is also accompanied by living and accommodation costs which are another hurdle exaggerated by unfortunate exchange rates. Moreover, undergraduate programs tuition fee in Dublin City University also differs vastly on the basis of the qualification or course that you wish to undertake and consequently the best option for you is to do some research on your own in looking up tuition fees here.

If a rough estimate is drawn, however, then it can be concluded that most programs at DCU would charge €15,000 per annum in terms of the undergraduate tuition fee.

Educational Requirements

Considering the academic integrity of Dublin City University, entrance requirements for most programmes is likely to be high and would incorporate performing well within your prior bachelor’s education and beyond it as well.

While details for programmes differ and consequently should be found out about via the university’s website, a general set of guidelines for admissions purposes can be found below.

  • Students who score 70% or more in their bachelor’s degree either in engineering, science, arts or commerce are more likely to get into. Another important criterion is the number of arrears or backlogs that you may have. Usually, Trinity College Dublin gives admission to students with less than 5 arrears.
  • Candidates with relevant work experience may have an added advantage.
  • Entry Requirement for Dublin City University (Percentage or CGPA) - 60% or 6.0 CGPA
  • IELTS Requirement for Dublin City University - 6.5 with no section below 6.0
  • TOEFL Requirement for Dublin City University - 92
  • PTE Requirement for Dublin City University - 63

Undergraduate Entry requirements for an academically demanding institution such as the Dublin City University are relatively high usually and consequently should always be strived for appropriately during the admissions process.

Admission at DCU for international students, may be another undergraduate English requirement at Dublin City University. This is an added hurdle for international students to cross alongside the ones mentioned below.

  • School leaving or transfer certificate alongside other relevant details of final results.
  • Candidates with relevant work experience may have an added advantage.
