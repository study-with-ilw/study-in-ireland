University of Limerick

MSc in Health Informatics MSc in Business Analytics MEng in Computer Engineering MEng in Information and Network Security MEng in Computer and Communications Systems MSc in Project Management Master of Artificial Intelligence

The University of Limerick is an academic institution in Ireland which was granted university status in 1989.

UL's emphasis on research work is another distinguishing characteristic of the university. Its focus is not limited to the development of an academic perspective within students, however. Instead, students are inculcated with skills which are professionally relevant and needed in Ireland's present socio-economic context.

The infrastructure available, alongside the expertise of the academic faculty at the University of Limerick, ensure that the quality of its undergraduate and postgraduate programmes is unrivalled. These are further benefited by the institute’s unprecedented teaching style and modern technology that it makes available to its students.

University Ranking

University of Limerick holds following Rankings :

  • 6th in Ireland in the QS World University Rankings 2018
  • A global top 550 World University in the latest QS World University Ranking 2018
  • 16th in Green Metric Rankings of World Universities 2015
  • Amongst the Top 200 Most International Universities in the world by Times Higher Education in 2015

Tuition Fees

Master's degree course fee at the University of Limerick for international students is higher than that for domestic students and for students from the European Union.

Approximately, full-time programs tuition fees at the University of Limerick for most courses should range from €11,616 to €50,038.

Educational Requirements

Postgraduate English requirement at the University of Limerick through any of the administered English Proficiency Tests.

Candidates with relevant work experience may have an added advantage.

  • Students who score 70% or more in their bachelor’s degree either in engineering, science, arts or commerce are more likely to get into. Another important criterion is the number of arrears or backlogs that you may have. Usually, the University of Limerick gives admissions to students with less than 5 arrears.
  • IELTS Requirement for University of Limerick: 6.5 with no section below 6.0
  • TOEFL Requirement for University of Limerick: 90
  • PTE Requirement for University of Limerick: 63 with no section below 59

Research Details

Research or PhD at the University of Limerick is a highly reputable and challenging avenue. Not only does it effectively guarantee career progression but it also explores highly contextual and relevant issues and is completely supported by the UL. Its present status as a high research focused institution was established via concerted efforts on the part of officials to bring together various disciplines in the process of discovering fundamental breakthroughs. This interdisciplinary approach towards research has led to the development of the following avenues. Materials and Surface Science, Information and Communication Technique, Bioengineering and Biosciences, Energy and Sustainable Environment, Applied Mathematics and Study of Knowledge in Society.
