Limerick Institute of Technology

Limerick Institute of Technology (LIT) is an institution of higher education in Limerick, Ireland.

LIT offers courses at Level 6 (certificate) through Level 10 (PhD) whilst also catering for craft apprentices and adult and continuing education. The Institute has twice been named as The Sunday Times Institute of Technology of the Year in The Sunday Times University Guide, firstly in 2008 and again in 2013

University Ranking

Limerick Institute of Technology has following Rankings :

  • Twice it has been named as The Sunday Times Institute of Technology of the year. It was in 2008 and then again in 2013.
  • Webometrics has ranked the Limerick Institute of Technology 18th in Ireland and 4477 in the world.
  • Uni Rank has also ranked it on 13 in the nation along with 3673 in the world.
  • In terms of Student Satisfaction LIT has got a 4.1 rating out of 5.

Tuition Fees

Postgraduate:

The cost of studying at LIT differs from course to course. Every Master’s degree course fee in Limerick Institute of Technology depends on the level of practical and classroom participation. The fee for international students starts from €10,750 to 18,500. All the Master’s programs tuition fee at LIT is €10,750 .

Undergraduate:

The bachelor’s degree course fee in LIT is divided into two parts. One is the tuition which starts at €6,750 and the higher range is €7,250. Apart from this, there is the student contribution fee which is a part of the Undergraduate tuition fee in Limerick Institute of Technology. This fee has been kept at €3,000.

Educational Requirements

Postgraduate:

  • Entry Requirement for Limerick Institute of Technology (Percentage or CGPA) - 60% or 6.0 CGPA
  • IELTS Requirement for Limerick Institute of Technology - 6.5 with no section below 5.5
  • TOEFL Requirement for Limerick Institute of Technology - 79
  • PTE Requirement for Limerick Institute of Technology - 59

Undergratuate:

  • The Undergraduate IELTS requirement in Limerick Institute of Technology is a score of 5.5 and above.
  • TOEFL requirement to gain entry into the institution is a minimum of 220 on the written. Plus, a score of 550 on the Internet test is also required.
  • Other eligible tests for Undergraduate English requirement in Limerick Institute of Technology are University of Cambridge test, GCE O-Level English Language. Also, there is the GCSE English Language test, Cambridge Proficiency Test, Cambridge Advanced and ARELS Oral Examinations. A student can pass any of these tests to secure their admittance into the LIT.

Research Details

LIT focuses on research excellence with impact on industrial leadership and societal challenges through innovative, applied research in partnership with indigenous and international industrial partners, which is focused on existing and emerging areas of expertise within LIT.

Research within LIT occurs through 14 dedicated centres and groups encompassing a wide variety of disciplines from Fine Art and Design, to Social Sciences, Biotechnology, Interactive Systems, Energy Management, and Sustainable Development. The research centres and groups all have a focus on developing niche areas of expertise to attract international researchers and external funding to provide innovative products and process for LITs industry partners and develop postgraduate research opportunities.

In parallel, the LIT Graduate Studies and Research Office plays a pivotal role in facilitating the academic progression and career development of LIT’s Masters (L9) and Ph.D. (L10) postgraduate students.
