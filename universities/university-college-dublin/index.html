University College Dublin (UCD).

Master of Business Administration (MBA) MSc in Business Analytics MSc in Computer Science MSc in Social Data Analytics MSc in Data & Computational Science

University College Dublin has made unique and great contributions to the creation of modern Ireland. It has helped improve the Irish society across every sphere of activity. The standards of the university have improved vastly in recent years and it is ranked within the top 1% of all institutions for higher education.

Established in 1854, the University has a student body of over 34,000 students with over 8,000 international students drawn from 139 countries. Students are attracted to UCD by the strength of its teaching and research, by the programme portfolio (UCD offers Ireland’s widest range of programmes which are recognised and valued by academic institutions and employers throughout the world) and the holistic experience which studying in UCD offers.

#tutionFeesDetails

The master’s programs tuition fee in UCD includes all the course work and tuition fees. The postgraduate tuition fee in UCD depends much on the facilities and training offered by the faculty. The graduate programs tuition fee in University College Dublin for the subjects varies every year and the correct information should be collected only from the official website.

Average Tuition Fee for University College Dublin is €19,200.

#educationRequirements

Another important criterion is the number of arrears or backlogs that you may have. Usually, University College Cork gives admissions to students with less than 5 arrears.

Research Details

Getting a PhD in University College Dublin is one of the most valued qualifications in the country. The college has been fully committed to the key role it plays in the innovation ecosystem by enabling growth and contributing to the social, economic and cultural development. Research takes place in a lot of innovative fields like culture, society and economy, health, energy, environment, ICT, agri-food and others. The university is driving discovery and innovation within the community of world-class researchers. The university nurtures and supports the high-tech and knowledge-intensive start-ups.
