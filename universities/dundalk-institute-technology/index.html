Dundalk Institute of Technology (DKIT)

MSc in Agricultural Biotechnology MSc in Computing MSc in Renewable Energy Systems MBS in Entrepreneurship and Marketing MA/MSc in Music Technology

Dundalk Institute of Technology (DKIT) has earned a reputation as the leading higher education provider in the North East of Ireland. The DkIT campus which is a 90-acre site, situated in Dundalk, County Louth is positioned halfway between the two largest cities on the island of Ireland, Belfast and Dublin (each approximately 80 kilometers away).

At DkIT you are offered the opportunity to build your qualifications over time, from Higher Certificate through to Degree, Masters and Doctorate Level. The College is committed to ensuring that students are afforded the opportunity to develop their full potential in a professional and supportive environment.

The college has four Schools, and each School has several Departments offering a wide range of courses. You can also study part-time at the Lifelong Learning Centre.

College is not just about studying, lectures, assignments, project work and exams. College is a place to call home, it’s about discovering new interests, and it’s about communities, friendships and opportunities.

Tuition Fees

The master’s degree course fee in Dundalk Institute of Technology varies according to the courses that you opt for. The students have to pay this fee annually through the two-year degree program. The above-discussed EU Fees is for full-time degree programs. The tuition fee of a masters degree in DkIT for part-time courses becomes 50% that of the full-time course.The minimum tuition fee for DkIT is €12,000.

Educational Requirements

Postgraduate Entry Requirement for International Students in Dundalk Institute of Technology

  • The candidate must possess an NQAI Level Eight award or equivalent qualification (at least Grade 2 level, 2nd Class Honours) in the concerned field
  • The candidates must have excellent command over English and to prove this they have to score good marks in TOEFL, IELTS or Equivalent exams.
  • Grade B2 equivalent on CEFR
  • Entry Requirement for Dundalk Institute of Technology (Percentage or CGPA) - 60% or 6.0 CGPA
  • IELTS Requirement for Dundalk Institute of Technology - 6.5 with no section below 5.5
  • TOEFL Requirement for Dundalk Institute of Technology - 79
  • PTE Requirement for Dundalk Institute of Technology - 59

Undergraduate Entry Requirement for International Students in Dundalk Institute of Technology

  • 5 Grades at H7/ O6 in Leaving Certificate
  • AND English Grade H7/ O6
  • AND Mathematics Grade O6/F2/H7
  • OR Irish Grade H7/ O6
  • The minimum Undergraduate IELTS requirement in DkIT for Honours Degree (Level 8) is 6.0
  • The minimum Undergraduate IELTS requirement in DkIT for Ordinary Degree (Level 7) is 5.5
