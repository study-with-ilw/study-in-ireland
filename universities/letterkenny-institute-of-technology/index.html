Letterkenny Institute of Technology (LYIT) fosters one of the most progressive learning environments in Ireland.It's reach is both local and international; attracting a diverse student body of more than 4,000 students from Ireland and 31 countries across the globe. Students choose LYIT because of LYIT's unique ethos that harnesses academic excellence with career-focused practical experience. It’s an approach that positions their students for future success in line with their aspirations.

LYIT also foster close relationships with the wider local community. Their engagement and partnerships with indigenous and international businesses leaders strengthens the student’s prospects in tandem with the prosperity of the region’s economy.LYIT's modern integrated campuses in Letterkenny and Killybegs aren’t just gateways to a bright future, but to one of the most breathtaking corners of the world - with Donegal named National Geographic’s ‘Coolest Place on the Planet 2017’.

Tuition Fees

Postgraduate:

The tuition fee for postgraduate programs in LyIT varies based on the course or program that we choose, the EU fees of various master degree programs that Letterkenny Institute of Technology offers can't be explained here, as they vary with a small change in the subject of discipline you choose.

For the candidates who don't satisfy the EU Fees criteria will be accountable for Non-EU Fees. As of now, as per the latest updates, the full-time Postgraduate tuition fees in Letterkenny Institute of Technology for Non-EU candidates is 10000 Euros.

Undergraduate:

The candidates from non-European Union will have to pay a fee of 9000 Euros each year throughout the entire duration of their preferred degree program.

Educational Requirements

Postgraduate:

  • The minimum Postgraduate IELTS requirement in LyIT is 6.5 with no band below 5.5
  • The minimum Postgraduate TOEFL requirement in LyIT is 79 with the least score in each segment of 15.
  • The minimum Postgraduate PTE requirement in LyIT is 59.
  • The candidates possessing any of the equivalent English proficiency qualifications will be eligible to apply for postgraduate programs in Letterkenny Institute of Technology.

Undergraduate:

  • The minimum undergraduate IELTS requirement in LyIT is 5.5 with no band below 5.0
  • The minimum undergraduate TOEFL requirement in LyIT is 70 with a minimum score in each segment of 15
  • The minimum undergraduate PTE requirement in LyIT is 42
  • The candidates must have completed their XII standard or High School Diploma with a minimum score of 60% in English and Mathematics.

Research Details

Letterkenny Institute of Technology’s Research Strategy 2015/16 - 2019/20 confirms its significant national profile for excellence in higher education through the pursuit of an ambitious research development agenda informed by public policy, strong regional engagement, and a fundamental commitment to a student-centred ethos.

The core of the LYIT Research Strategy is to contribute to this mission by creating a research and development environment that brings researchers and students together with start-ups, regional industries and local communities.

LYIT's Research Strategy responds to four key drivers:

The desire to (re)commit to educational excellence at undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

The imperative to deliver knowledge based benefits to the North West Region of Ireland.

The need to support ongoing academic enterprise and business incubation initiatives.

The desire to disseminate research findings at international conferences and in peer reviewed journals.

  • Computing
  • Business
