National University of Ireland Galway (NUIG)

MSc in Cloud ComputingMSc in CybersecurityMSc in Data AnalyticsMSc in EntrepreneurshipMSc in FinanceMSc in FinTechMA in Human Resource ManagementMSc in International BusinessMSc in ManagementMSc in Marketing

The National University of Ireland Galway (NUI Galway) is located near the city of Galway on the western coast of Ireland and was founded in 1845. More than 18,000 students are currently enrolled, over 3,000 of which are international, and NUI Galway is ranked in the top 1% of universities worldwide.

The University is well known for programmes in computer science, English language and literature, history, pharmacy, pharmacology, and law, all of which are listed in the QS World Rankings by Subject. The University is a member of the Coimbra group of long-established European universities and has a number of partnerships with other global universities as well.

NUI is a leading university in Ireland for research and academic excellence. The Insight Centre for Data Analytics at NUI is the leading data analytics research centre in Ireland, while its Health Promotion Research Centre is recognised by the World Health Organisation and CURAM. The Centre for Medical Device Research is the number one research institute of its kind in Ireland. NUI Galway has over 50 undergraduate and 400 postgraduate courses available to choose from.

University Ranking

NUI Galway is the only Irish university to maintain and increase its ranking in two of the main World University Rankings (i.e. times Higher Education and QS World Rankings).

  • 243rd in the QS World University Rankings 2017/2018
  • 4 of NUI’s Professors were named as the world’s most highly cited researchers in november 2016 by clarivite
  • 20 subjects at NUI were ranked in the top tier globally as per the QS World Subject Rankings

Tuition FeesDetails

Master’s degree course fee in NCI covers the cost of studies along with charges for tuition, registration, examinations, graduation and supervision. Postgraduate tuition fee in NCI is €14,500.

Bachelors degree course fee in NCI tends to vary as per the courses that are chosen. Bachelors programs tuition fee in NCI is around €10,000. The undergraduate tuition fee in NCI does not include charges of food and accommodation.

Educational Requirements

  • It is important for all the international students to meet both the academic requirements and the postgraduate English requirements in NCI. International students who do not have English as their first language should have the right score in a duly approved examination in English language prior to registering on any academic course from the college.
  • Candidates with relevant work experience may have an added advantage.
  • Entry Requirement for National College of Ireland (Percentage or CGPA) - 60% or 6.0 CGPA
  • IELTS Requirement for National College of Ireland - 6.0 with no section below 5.5
  • TOEFL Requirement for National College of Ireland - 79
  • PTE Requirement for National College of Ireland - 59

Research Details

NUI Galway is world-renowned as an expert research-led University. They emphasise inter-disciplinary research programmes, and collaborations with industry partners in areas of strategic importance, both regionally and nationally.

There is a vibrant research and innovation ecosystem at this university. This dynamic community attracts researchers, academics, entrepreneurs and students of the highest calibre.

Workplace learning, technology-enhanced learning and lifelong learning are pillars that represent the National College of Ireland and its current and historical field of interest.
