Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT)

Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT) is one of the longest established (1970), largest and most academically respected Institutes of Technology in Ireland. It is an autonomous Higher Education Institution operating within the Irish National Qualifications Network. This institute is a major provider of education in South Eastern Ireland. Plus, Waterford Institute of Technology is quick to absorb the ever-changing needs of the city and the country on a whole. This allows them to process their study propaganda as per the new demands of the industry.

The range of academic programmes is exceptionally extensive, ranging from Higher Certificate right through to post-doctoral research and covering the Humanities, Health and Nursing, Science and Informatics, Engineering and Architecture, Business and Education. The awards from WIT are recognised worldwide and graduates of the Institute occupy senior positions in major companies in Ireland, the EU and further afield.

The Careers Centre at WIT is the main point of contact for

  • Students and graduates, clear and focused on planning for and securing productive employment
  • Critical services and advice to students on career-related resources to assist them in today’s competitive global employment market

University Ranking

Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT) has following Rankings :

  • Webometrics has accorded WIT 7th best higher education body in Ireland in 2008. This ranking was out of 32 total higher education bodies in the country.
  • Unirank has also given 10th rank to the institute.

Tuition Fees

Postgraduate:

For master’s degree course fee in WIT, there are different fee structures for the international and the national students.The full time postgraduate tuition fee in Waterford Institute of Technology for international students is €6,500/year. You can choose from among the 1 year, 2-year, 3-year or 4-year courses. However, for the Irish and the EU based student graduate programs tuition fee in WIT is kept at €4,500/year.

Undergraduate:

The ordinary bachelors degree fee in Waterford Institute of Technology is €10,250.This may vary according to the course selected.

Educational Requirements

  • Entry Requirement for Waterford Institute of Technology (Percentage or CGPA): 60% or 6.0 CGPA
  • IELTS Requirement for Waterford Institute of Technology: 6.0 with no section below 5.5
  • TOEFL Requirement for Waterford Institute of Technology: 80
  • PTE Requirement for Waterford Institute of Technology: 55

Research Details

Waterford Institute of Technology is committed to being a research-led Institute, recognised for its distinctive, high quality centres of research. WIT strategy is to create substantial centres of excellence for world-class research in which individual scholars and research groups are given maximum freedom to pursue their investigations. The aim here is to set up such institutions of research that will be the front runner in advance and a deep understanding of all the areas of academics. By enrolling for PhD in WIT you will be connected to a world of new research abilities and will be able to acquaint with the global research community. WIT has curated a unique ecosystem of research and development. Whereby individuals and organizations from diverse fields come together under one roof to work in collaboration.
