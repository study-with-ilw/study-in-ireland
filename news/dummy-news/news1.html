Global Accreditation Awarded to University of Limerick’s Kemmy Business School

Published 05 September 2019

University of Limerick’s Kemmy Business School (KBS) has been awarded worldwide accreditation and will be listed in the top 5% of international business schools recognised by The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). Ireland is topping the charts in the list of most preferred study destination for international students in the context of quality education, high-ranking universities, warm welcome by universities, unparalleled academic and career support and life experiences. KBS being recognised as International by AACSB International is great news for students across the globe. Degrees offered at KBS will be treated as globally recognised that will give the student a chance to get an international degree from a highly reputed school in Ireland. KBS is one of biggest business schools offering first-class business education with a wide range of business and management education programmes that build a solid foundation needed to acquire the necessary analytical and business skills and in-depth knowledge of various aspects of international business, both on campus and at external locations throughout Ireland. The school provides excellent teaching, has good infrastructure, equipment, and facilities, has dynamic and stimulating research-led training environment, and offers high student satisfaction with competent teachers and professors that are always available and eager to help. Also, KBS accommodates students of all backgrounds and interests and has a diverse culture that is most needed by an international student. At KBS, the Alumni and student bodies seem to be offering a lot of support and a great chance to network with alumni spread all over the world. As the school is continuously updating and upgrading its resources, this will make students future-ready. Students learn team spirit, exchange of ideas, and studying in a vibrant atmosphere enhances their growth. Ireland is a land full of possibilities, and the research focused modules, programme assessment, lower than average dropout rate and reputation for academic excellence in the world has encouraged students in huge numbers across the globe to come and study here.
